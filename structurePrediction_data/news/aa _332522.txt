The iBOXX.CDX Consortium is Pleased to Announce the Names Comprising

iBOXX.CDX.NA.IG.2



SECTION: FINANCIAL NEWS



LENGTH: 1714 words



DATELINE: NEW YORK March 19





The iBOXX.CDX consortium today announced the composition of the new 125-name

portfolio known as iBOXX.CDX.NA.IG.2. The index begins trading on March 22,

2004, and matures on September 20, 2009. Coupon setting, which was completed at

12:00 Noon on Thursday, March 18, 2004, will be officially announced to the

broad market today, Friday, March 19 at 5:00pm.



The complete 125-name portfolio is listed below. The amendments consist of the

removal of six companies which are replaced by six that had not previously been

included. They are:

About iBOXX.CDX



A 125-name tradable index of credit default swaps ("CDS") on U.S. companies

organized by a consortium of leading CDS market makers. The consortium is

currently comprised of 12 members: ABN AMRO, Barclays Capital, Bear Stearns,

Citigroup, Credit Suisse First Boston, Deutsche Bank, Goldman Sachs, HSBC Bank

USA, Lehman Brothers, Merrill Lynch, UBS and Wachovia. The index is administered

by iBOXX, an independent credit index provider with multiple contributor

pricing. The iBOXX CDX index is listed on Bloomberg under the symbol "CDX CDS

<corp>".
