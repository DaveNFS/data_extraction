investment community in an all-new interview with  http://www.wallst.net/  .

Interview highlights include detailed discussions on the following topics:

   * partnership with Oilexco North Sea Ltd. (TSX: OIL)



   * potential production of the company's working interest properties



   * increasing exploration and development



   * why now is "the time" for investors to "take a look" at the company



   * upcoming development milestones for investors to watch for







To hear the interview in its entirety, and to read an in-depth report on the

company, visit

http://www.wallst.net/superstocks/superstocks_profile.asp?ticker=gulsf
