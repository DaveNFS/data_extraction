LENGTH: 6523 words



DATELINE: NEW YORK, Nov. 15, 2010





NEW YORK, Nov. 15, 2010 /PRNewswire/ --Reportlinker.com announces that a new

market research report is available in its catalogue:



Global Insulated Wire And Cable Industry



http://www.reportlinker.com/p092515/Global-Insulated-Wire-And-Cable-Industry.htm

l



This report analyzes the worldwide markets for Insulated Wire and Cable in US$

Million by the following Product Segments: Building Wire and Cable, Electronic

Wire and Cable, Power Wire and Cable, Telecommunications, Data Wire and Cable,

and Other Insulated Wires and Cables. The report provides separate comprehensive

analytics for the US, Canada, Japan, Europe, Asia Pacific, Middle East, and

Latin America. Annual estimates and forecasts are provided for each region for

the period 2007 through 2015. Also, a seven-year historic analysis is provided

for these markets. The report profiles 273 companies including many key and

niche players such as ABL Electronic Supplies, Inc., ADC Telecommunications,

Inc., AEI Cables Ltd., Alcan Cable, Alcatel-Lucent SA, Alcoa, Inc., Amphenol

Corp., Asia Pacific Wire & Cable Corporation Ltd., Belden Inc., Brand-Rex Ltd.,

Cable USA Inc, Carlisle Interconnect Technologies, Cerro Wire LLC, Coleman

Cable, Inc., CommScope, Inc., Corning Incorporated, Delphi Corporation, Draka

Holding N.V., Draka Limited, Electrical Components International, Encore Wire

Corporation, Finolex Cables Ltd., Fujikura Ltd., Furukawa Electric Company

Limited, General Cable Corporation, Phelps Dodge International Corp., Hitachi

Cable Ltd, LaBarge, Inc., LS Cable Limited, Superior Essex Incorporated, MOLEX,

Inc., NEXANS, NKT Cables Group GmbH, Olex Cables Limited, Prysmian Cables &

Systems Ltd., Rea Magnet Wire Company, Inc., Southwire Company, American

Insulated Wire Corp., Sumitomo Electric Industries Ltd., Tele-Fonika Kable

Handel S.A.., and The Okonite Company. Market data and analytics are derived

from primary and secondary research. Company profiles are mostly extracted from

URL research and reported select online sources.
