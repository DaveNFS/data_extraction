George S. Barrett, Chairman and CEO, Cardinal Health, Inc., Vikram Malhotra,

Chairman of the Americas, McKinsey & Company, Elected to the Board of Trustees

of The Conference Board



LENGTH: 224 words



DATELINE: NEW YORK, Dec. 13, 2010





NEW YORK, Dec. 13, 2010 /PRNewswire/ -- George S. Barrett, chairman and CEO,

Cardinal Health, Inc. and Vikram Malhotra, chairman of the Americas, McKinsey &

Company, have been elected to the board of trustees of The Conference Board

(http://www.conference-board.org), the global business research and membership

association.



Dr. Josef Ackermann, Chairman of the Management Board and the Group Executive

Committee, Deutsche Bank AG, Alan M. Dachs, President and CEO of the Fremont

Group, and Klaus Kleinfeld, Chairman and CEO of Alcoa Inc., have also been

re-elected to the board of trustees.



A complete list of The Conference Board's board of trustees for 2010-2011 is

available at:



http://www.conference-board.org/about/index.cfm?id=1979



About The Conference Board



The Conference Board is a global, independent business membership and research

association working in the public interest. Our mission is unique: To provide

the world's leading organizations with the practical knowledge they need to

improve their performance and better serve society. The Conference Board is a

non-advocacy, not-for-profit entity holding 501 (c) (3) tax-exempt status in the

United States.
