Research and Markets: Examine Forecasts to 2010 for the Global Market Of Vehicle

Electrical Wiring Systems



LENGTH: 463 words



DATELINE: DUBLIN, Ireland May 19





DUBLIN, Ireland, May 19  /PRNewswire/ -- Research and Markets (

http://www.researchandmarkets.com/reports/c17617 ) has announced the addition of

A Global Market Review Of Vehicle Electrical Wiring Systems -- Forecasts To 2010

-- 2nd Edition, 2005 Edition to their offering.



(Logo:  http://www.newscom.com/cgi-bin/prnh/20040820/RESEARCH  )



In this second edition review of vehicle electrical wiring systems, we extend

the analysis from our respected first edition by providing market share data for

West Europe, North America, Japan and worldwide, plus give you market value

forecasts for wiring harnesses through 2010. As well as a technical review,

detailing recent and future advances and trends, chapter three provides you with

profiles of the 8 major manufacturers dominating this sector, namely Alcoa

Fujikura, Delphi, Intedis, Lear, Leoni, Sumitomo Wiring Systems, Valeo and

Yazaki.
