Million. The report provides separate comprehensive analytics for the US,

Canada, Japan, Europe, Asia-Pacific, Latin America, and Rest of World. Annual

six-year historic analysis is provided for these markets. The report profiles

124 companies including many key and niche players such as Alcoa, Inc., Amcor

Packaging LLC, Rexam Plc, RPC Group Plc, SIG Combibloc Group, Silgan Holdings,

Inc., Southeastern Container, Inc., and Tetra Laval International SA. Market

data and analytics are derived from primary and secondary research. Company

profiles are mostly extracted from URL research and reported select online

sources.
