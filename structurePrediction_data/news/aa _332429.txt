SAE International and Alcoa Quality Institute to Offer OnlineCourses



SECTION: NATIONAL DESK



LENGTH: 176 words



DATELINE: WARRENDALE, Pa.,  Aug. 15





The Society of Automotive Engineers and Alcoa are partnering to offer a

comprehensive selection of self-paced online courses known collectively as the

Alcoa Quality Institute.



Alcoa, long regarded as a leader in process management, is a supporter of this

e-learning program, which consists of the same courses used to train its own

professionals.



Offering 23 courses and totaling 278 hours of learning, the AQI covers both

basic and advanced concepts. Examples of courses include:



-- Concepts of Control Charts



-- Internal Auditing



-- Problem Solving



AQI courses are flexible enough to suit many types of organizations and

individuals. The program will help students establish a consistent methodology

for proactive process management and enhance their knowledge of Alcoa principles

and skills.



For a list of AQI online courses and information on how to register, visit SAE

International online at http://www.sae.org/aqi.
