Aleris Names Kelly Thomas Vice President, Global Risk Management



LENGTH: 247 words



DATELINE: BEACHWOOD, Ohio, Sept. 29





BEACHWOOD, Ohio, Sept. 29 /PRNewswire/ -- Aleris International, Inc. has named

Kelly Thomas vice president, Global Risk Management, effective October 1, 2010.

Thomas, currently director, Risk Europe, succeeds Mary Lou Southwood, who leaves

Aleris upon completion of her engagement to reengineer the global risk

management process.



Thomas has nearly 20 years of financial and commercial leadership experience.

Prior to joining Aleris in 2010, Thomas was vice president, Alcoa Materials

Management Europe. Before Alcoa, she held management positions at IBM and

Reynolds Metals.



"Kelly has played a key role in developing our enhanced and expanded global risk

organization at Aleris," said Sean Stack, Aleris executive vice president and

chief financial officer. "Her metal, commercial and risk experience will enable

Aleris to continue to drive best practices into our global organization."



Thomas has a B.S. in Finance from the University of Virginia.



About Aleris



Aleris International, Inc. is a privately-held, global leader in aluminum rolled

products and extrusions, aluminum recycling and specification alloy production.

Headquartered in Beachwood, Ohio, the company operates more than 40 production

facilities in the Americas, Europe and Asia. For more information, visit

www.aleris.com.
