Omni-Lite Industries Receives a Significant U.S. Military Order



LENGTH: 304 words



DATELINE: CERRITOS, CA, March 2, 2012





OML-TSX VENTURE



CERRITOS, CA, March 2, 2012 /PRNewswire/ - Omni-Lite Industries Canada Inc. is

pleased to announce that it has received a new development contract for a

significant program for the U.S. Military. This award stems from the success

that Omni-Lite has demonstrated in several previous programs for this large

customer.



"This new contract is a very significant development for Omni-Lite and

compliments the four orders announced in the press releases of January 27, 2012

and March 1, 2012," stated Allen W. Maxin, President. "This order further

demonstrates the successful match of the technology that the Company has

developed over the last four to five years to the needs of the U.S. Military.

Omni-Lite is committed to continuing the development of these technologies in

concert with our customers. Omni-Lite is discussing the ensuing production

requirements of these programs with our customers."



Omni-Lite Industries Canada Inc. is a rapidly growing high technology company

that develops and manufactures precision components utilized by several Fortune

500 Companies including Boeing, Airbus, Alcoa, Ford, Caterpillar, Borg Warner,

Chrysler, the U.S. Military, Nike, and adidas.
