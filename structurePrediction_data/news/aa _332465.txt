Institutional Investor Magazine Ranks America's Best CFOs



LENGTH: 985 words



DATELINE: NEW YORK Feb. 14





NEW YORK, Feb. 14  /PRNewswire/ -- Today's top chief financial officers don't

just count beans. They help plant, raise and harvest them too.



For the second year running, Institutional Investor magazine asked portfolio

managers and brokerage firm research analysts to name the best American CFOs in

62 industries. Many of the winners of II's second annual "America's Best CFOs"

ranking -- which appears in the magazine's February issue, released today -- are

making critical business decisions and helping set corporate strategy, taking on

the role once filled by chief operating officers.



"Excellence in financial functions is still at the core of the CFO's job,

especially with the added burden of ensuring Sarbanes-Oxley Act compliance,"

says Michael Carroll, Editor of Institutional Investor. "But many of today's top

CFOs are taking on additional duties as business operators."
