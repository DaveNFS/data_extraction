Omni-Lite Industries receives new orders that include all divisions of the

company



LENGTH: 312 words



DATELINE: CERRITOS, CA, April 5, 2012





OML-TSX VENTURE



CERRITOS, CA, April 5, 2012 /PRNewswire/ - Omni-Lite Industries Canada Inc. is

pleased to announce that it has received a new production contract for one of

the many programs that it has underway for a U.S. Military customer. The Company

began deliveries under this contract approximately two weeks ago. "This award

stems from the excellent results obtained during the first article tests in

March of 2012," stated Allen W. Maxin, President.



Omni-Lite Industries is also pleased to announce that it has received several

new orders that include all divisions of the Company. The total value of these

new orders is approximately $712,000 US. Of these new orders approximately 37%

are in the Sports and Recreation division, 24% are in the Aerospace division,

24% are in the Military division and 15% are in the Specialty Automotive

division.



The Company would also like to announce that revenues for the first quarter were

approximately $1,353,000 US.



Omni-Lite Industries Canada Inc. is a rapidly growing high technology company

that develops and manufactures precision components utilized by several Fortune

500 Companies including Boeing, Airbus, Alcoa, Ford, Caterpillar, Borg Warner,

Chrysler, the U.S. Military, Nike, and adidas.
