Jerome de Truchis to Join Align Aerospace France as General Manager - Europe



LENGTH: 314 words



DATELINE: COLLEGIEN, France, April 10, 2012





Align Aerospace France, SAS ("Align" or the "Company"), a leading provider of

hardware components, logistics and supply chain solutions in the aerospace and

defense industries, announced today the appointment of Jerome de Truchis as

General Manager - Europe.



(Logo: http://photos.prnewswire.com/prnh/20120410/LA84423LOGO)



M. de Truchis most recently served as the head of the Industrial Technologies

France Sales & Service team of Ingersoll Rand. M. de Truchis had previously

served in a variety of positions with Alcoa Fastening Systems and its

predecessor company Fairchild Fasteners. Commenting on M. de Truchis'

appointment, Raynard D. Benvenuti, Chairman of the Board of Directors of Align

and Align's Interim CEO commented, "Jerome brings a wealth of experience in the

aerospace fastener business to Align Europe. He will champion our team to

dramatically expand our European aerospace and defense presence. Align's Board

and shareholders are extremely pleased to have someone with Jerome's significant

industry experience and proven leadership capabilities taking on this important

role."



M. de Truchis stated, "I am pleased to be taking on this pivotal position with

Align, and I look forward to leading Align's growth in Europe."



About Align:



Align is a global provider of hardware components, logistics and supply chain

solutions in the global aerospace and defense industries. Between 1998 and 2011,

the Company operated as Pentacon, Eurofast SAS, and Anixter Aerospace Hardware.

Align began independent operations in August 2011 as a result of the divestiture

of the business by Anixter International to Greenbriar Equity Group.
