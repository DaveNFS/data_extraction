Dahlman Rose & Co. to Host Second Annual Global Metals, Mining & Materials

Conference



LENGTH: 791 words



DATELINE: NEW YORK, Nov. 9, 2011





Dahlman Rose & Company, LLC, a leading investment bank specializing in the

global natural resources supply chain, will host its 2nd Annual Global Metals,

Mining & Materials Conference on November 16-17, 2011, in New York City. More

than 60 top-tier metals & mining, precious metals, coal mining and agricultural

and commodity chemical companies will present at the two-day conference. Keynote

speakers include Richard Adkerson, President and Chief Executive Officer of

Freeport-McMoRan Copper & Gold (NYSE: FCX); Dennis Gartman, Editor and Publisher

of The Gartman Letter; and John Surma, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of

U.S. Steel (NYSE: X).



"We are proud to host an extensive lineup of leading companies at our 2nd Annual

Global Metals, Mining & Materials Conference," said Kim Fennebresque, Chairman

and Chief Executive Officer of Dahlman Rose & Co. "This two-day event will

provide investors with an ideal platform to expand their industry relationships

and learn more about the key factors driving the resilient market that exists

for critical commodities on a global basis."



The format of the conference will consist of presentations and panel

discussions, as well as one-on-one meetings with company management. Companies,

by sector, scheduled to participate include:



METALS & MINING - AK Steel (NYSE: AKS), Alcoa Inc. (NYSE: AA), Allegheny

Technologies (NYSE: ATI), Arcelor Mittal (NYSE: MT), BHP Billiton (NYSE: BHP),

Cameco Corporation (NYSE: CCJ), Century Aluminum (NASDAQ: CENX), China Gerui

Advanced Materials Group (NASDAQ: CHOP); Cliffs Natural Resources (NYSE: CLF),

Denison Mines (NYSE: DNN), Freeport-McMoRan Copper & Gold (NYSE: FCX), General

Moly (NYSE: GMO), Globe Specialty Metals (NYSE: GSM), GrafTech (NYSE: GTI),

Kaiser Aluminum (NASDAQ: KALU), Materion (NYSE: MTRN), Mercator (TSX: ML),

Metals USA Holdings (NASDAQ: MUSA), Molycorp (NYSE: MCP), NCI Building Systems,

(NYSE: NCS), Orbite Aluminae Inc. (TSX: ORT), Quadra FNX Mining (TSX: QUX),

Reliance Steel & Aluminum (NYSE: RS), Stillwater Mining Company (NYSE: SWC),

SunCoke Energy (NYSE: SXC), Talison Lithium (TSX: TLH), Teck Resources (NYSE:

TCK), Thompson Creek Metals Company (NYSE: TC), Uranerz Energy Corporation

(NYSE: URZ), Uranium Energy Corporation (NYSE: UEC) and U.S. Steel Corp. (NYSE:

X).



PRECIOUS METALS - Agnico-Eagle Mines Limited (NYSE: AEM), Coeur d'Alene Mines

Corporation (NYSE: CDE), First Majestic Silver Corporation (NYSE: AG), Golden

Ltd. (NYSE: IVN), Kinross Gold Corporation (NYSE: KGC), Newmont Mining

Corporation (NYSE: NEM), NovaGold Resources (NYSE: NG), Pan American Silver

COAL MINING - Alpha Natural Resources (NYSE: ANR), Arch Coal (NYSE: ACI), CONSOL

COMMODITY CHEMICALS - Braskem (NYSE: BAK), Celanese (NYSE: CE), CF Industries

There will be a commodities lunch panel discussion at noon on November 17th

featuring representatives of CRU, Behre Dolbear & Company, Doyle Trading

Consultants LLC and Oceanic Iron Ore Corp.



The conference will be led by Anthony B. Rizzuto, Jr., Managing Director of

Equity Research - Metals & Mining; Anthony Young - Director, Metals & Mining;

Adam P. Graf, CFA, Director - Precious Metals & Mining; Daniel Scott, Director -

For further details regarding Dahlman Rose & Co.'s 2nd Annual Global Metals,

Mining & Materials Conference, please contact events@drco.com
