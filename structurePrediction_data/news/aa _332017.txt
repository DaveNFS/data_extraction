Release of Economic Study to Show Impact of U.S. Climate Legislation on American

Economy



LENGTH: 321 words



DATELINE: WASHINGTON, Dec. 1





WASHINGTON, Dec. 1 /PRNewswire-USNewswire/ -- The U.S. Climate Action

Partnership -- a coalition of major corporations and leading environmental

organizations -- will release the results of an economic study evaluating the

impact on the U.S. economy of potential legislation to reduce greenhouse gas

emissions. The study examines U.S. GDP and household income under legislation

modeled after USCAP's policy recommendations.
