Prestigious Magnus Award Finalists Announced by the M&A Advisor



LENGTH: 500 words



DATELINE: NEW YORK Oct. 20





NEW YORK, Oct. 20 /PRNewswire/ -- Excellence in M&A transactions over $1 billion

in deal size will be recognized at the 17th Annual Middle-Market Conference and

7th Annual Awards Gala on December 15th at the historic Hudson Theatre in NY.



"The Magnus Awards(TM) are awarded to the most elite players in the M&A field

today," said Roger Aguinaldo, CEO and Publisher of the M&A Advisor. "This year

we had a particularly exceptional pool of applicants and we are proud to

announce the finalists."



There will be two different Magnus Awards(TM) given out at the gala, Deal of the

Year and International Deal of the Year.



  Finalists for Deal of the Year 2008 are as follows:



     -- Clear Channel Communications' acquisition by Bain Capital and Thomas

        H. Lee Partners

        Nominated by Akin Gump Strauss Hauer & Feld LLP



     -- Hologic, Inc. acquires Cytyc Corporation

        Nominated by Brown Rudnick Berlack Israels LLP



     -- Northwest Airlines Corporation Chapter 11 proceedings

        Nominated by Cadwalader, Wickersham & Taft LLP



     -- Inverness Medical Innovations acquires Biosite, Inc.

        Nominated by Covington Associates



     -- GE Capital Solutions acquires Trustreet Properties, Inc.

        Nominated by Hogan & Hartson



     -- ENI Acquires Dominion's Offshore Gulf of Mexico Assets

        Nominated by Bracewell & Giuliani LLP



  Finalists for International Deal of the Year 2008 are:



     -- Merger of Sapa AB's aluminum extrusion business with Alcoa Inc.'s

        soft alloy aluminum extrusion business

        Nominated by Duane Morris LLP



     -- Mubadala Development Company (Abu Dhabi) investment in Carlyle Group

        Nominated by Akin Gump Strauss Hauer & Feld LLP



     -- Norilsk Nickel Group acquires LionOre Mining International Ltd.

        Nominated by Hogan & Hartson



     -- Consolidation of the Petrochemical Assets of Unipar and Petrobras

        for the formation of a Petrochemical Corporation in Brazil's South-

        Eastern Region

        Nominated by Leoni Siqueira Advogados



     -- Vimpelcom merger with Golden Telecom

        Nominated by Akin Gump Strauss Hauer & Feld LLP



     -- Flextronics International Ltd. Acquires Solectron Corporation

        Nominated by Curtis, Mallet-Prevost, Colt & Mosle LLP
