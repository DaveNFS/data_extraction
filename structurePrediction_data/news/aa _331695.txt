a CAGR of 6.57 percent over the period 2011-2015. One of the key factors

contributing to this market growth is the recovery of the Manufacturing sector.

The Industrial Casting market has also been witnessing the steady replacement of

ferrous castings with non-ferrous castings. However, the lack of effective

product differentiation could pose a challenge to the growth of this market.
