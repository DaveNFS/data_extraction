Clearwire Corporation (NASDAQ:CLWR): On Thursday, Sprint Nextel Corporation, who

owns 49% of Clearwire Corporation (NASDAQ:CLWR), confirmed that Japanese mobile

carrier Softbank is in talks to the company on "a potential substantial

investment" that could lead to a change of control. The deal "may or may not

occur," Dan Hesse, Chief Executive at Sprint, said in an email to employees.

Sprint said it will not comment further "unless and until an agreement is

reached." Shares of Clearwire shares rallied 70.77% to US$2.22 on the Softbank

rumor. Abnormal trading volume of Clearwire was found reaching 170.09 million

shares, 20 times heavier than usual day trading. Clearwire's 52-week range

remained US$0.83 to US$2.64 with its market capitalization touching US$3.24

billion. Its price, as of the latest close, was up 167% compared with the

52-week low and was 19% below the 52-week high. Insiderslab.com believes that it

is a clever way to check if insiders like CEOs, CFOs, and Directors in Clearwire

are starting to buy more company shares. See insider trade report for CLWR here.
