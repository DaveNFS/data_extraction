Randy R. Zeno Appointed New Dr. Miracle's CEO



LENGTH: 342 words



DATELINE: NEW YORK, Sept. 16





NEW YORK, Sept. 16 /PRNewswire/ -- Dr. Miracle's, a front-runner in the ethnic

personal care industry, announces that its Board of Directors has

newly-appointed Randy R. Zeno to the position of Chief Executive Officer.



(Photo: http://photos.prnewswire.com/prnh/20100916/NY66499 )



(Photo: http://www.newscom.com/cgi-bin/prnh/20100916/NY66499 )



Mr. Zeno, 49, joins Dr Miracle's from Reynolds Consumer Products, the leader in

consumer food wraps and bags where he was interim President as the business

transitioned from Alcoa to its new owner, the Rank Group. Prior to that, he was

Chief Marketing Officer for Reynolds Consumer Products. Randy brings significant

leadership experience in the consumer products industry with companies such as

Nabisco, Sara Lee, Cadbury Schweppes, International Home Foods and Birdseye. He

is a talented business pioneer, who, throughout his career, has exhibited the

ability to increase value through brand building, driving significant innovation

and growing revenue and profit.



"I intend to leverage the strong capabilities our company possesses along with

my background in growing companies towards building the Dr. Miracle's brand into

a personal care brand that better serves the needs of its devoted consumers,"

states Mr. Zeno.



Dr. Miracle's is a leading developer and marketer of quality hair care products

specifically formulated for the ethnic hair care market. The brand centers

around an innovative product line focused on efficacy backed by scientific

formulations but with a fun, unconventional and humorous marketing message. More

than 25 products are marketed under the Dr. Miracle's brand, all of which are

sold in drug stores and mass merchandisers nationwide, as well as in over 7,500

doors in the beauty and barber shop channel. Dr. Miracle's recently entered into

the skincare arena with the launch of My Goodbye Acne System, winner of the

"2008 Breakthrough Product of the Year" in the mass category by Women's Wear

Daily.
