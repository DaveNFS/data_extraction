Seat Pagine Gialle SpA announced that Seat PG Italia has renewed its partnership with Google Inc and will remain a Premium Partner of AdWords, the advertising programme that allows companies to promote themselves on the Google search engine and the Google Display network. A Premium Partner since 2009, Seat PG Italia has integrated Google AdWords into its PGclick portfolio of tools and offers personalized advertising solutions created specifically for each SME, identifying the right combination of parameters for each company.
