Yandex NV:Says that Yandex and Google have entered into a mutually beneficial strategic partnership agreement in respect of online display advertising sales.Says through this partnership, DoubleClick Bid Manager, Google's Demand-Side Platform will be connected to Yandex's Real Time Bidding system, while Yandex's Demand-Side Platform, AWAPS will join Google's RTB marketplace, DoubleClick AdExchange.Says the agreement only relates to display advertising, and does not cover text-based contextual advertising.Says technical integration is expected to take several months.?
