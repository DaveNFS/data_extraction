Pace Plc Updates on Acquisition Of Google Inc's Motorola Home Business

Pace Plc announced that Google Inc has reached agreement to sell its Motorola Home business. The Company was unable to reach an agreement with Google on terms that the Board believes would have been in the interests of Pace's shareholders. Accordingly, Pace has contacted the Financial Services Authority to request that the suspension of its securities from the Official List is lifted without delay. Pace expects the suspension to be lifted shortly.?
