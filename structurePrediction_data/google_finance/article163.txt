ICG Group Inc Announces Completion Of Sale Of Channel Intelligence To Google Inc

ICG Group Inc announced that the acquisition of one of its consolidated companies, Channel Intelligence, Inc. (CI), by Google Inc was consummated .ICG realized approximately $60.5 million of cash in connection with the transaction. A portion of ICG's proceeds were placed in escrow to satisfy potential indemnification claims under the purchase agreement; subject to any such claims, the escrowed proceeds will be released to ICG on or around August 20, 2014.?
